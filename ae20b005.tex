
$T = (\dot m_a + \dot m_f) V_e - \dot m_a V_0 + (P_e - P_0)A_e$\\

where,\\
T = thrust\\
$\dot m_a$ = mass flow rate of air\\
$\dot m_f$ = mass flow rate of fuel\\
$V_e$ = exit velocity of flow\\
$V_0$ = incoming flow velocity\\
$P_e$ = exit pressure \\
$P_0$ = chamber pressure\\
$A_e$ = Nozzle exit area
